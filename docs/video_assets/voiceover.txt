Operations teams drown in telemetry, alerts, and fragmented workflows. BitsIO AgenticOps was built to turn Splunk data into explainable AI decisions with human control.
On Dashboard, teams see live incidents, confidence, approvals, and runtime status in one place.
In Incidents, every conclusion is transparent: evidence retrieval, correlation, reasoning, confidence, and policy checks. Nothing is a black box.
Before action, Decision Gate enforces human approval with clear approve and reject paths, creating immutable audit trails.
Approvals centralizes all pending decisions for fast, accountable review.
Monitoring gives platform health, latency, and runtime quality so teams trust what they see.
Telemetry Value Impact quantifies ingest waste, utilization gaps, and annual savings opportunities from real telemetry behavior.
Fraud Risk Agent correlates payment, identity, and approval anomalies into explainable risk recommendations with compliance context.
In Settings, teams switch runtime profiles between local and cloud modes, validate connections, and operate safely across environments.
